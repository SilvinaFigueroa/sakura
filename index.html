Cherry Blossom Highlights

Ueno Park

Experience the festive atmosphere at Ueno Park, where thousands of people gather to enjoy the pink canopy of cherry blossoms. Ideal for picnics and boat rides, it's a must-visit during sakura season.

Chidorigafuchi

Rent a boat and paddle through serene waters lined with cherry trees. Chidorigafuchi is a romantic spot, perfect for couples and tranquil photo opportunities.

Shinjuku Gyoen

Explore one of Tokyo’s largest and most popular parks to see a mix of late and early-blooming cherry trees. Shinjuku Gyoen offers spacious lawns and picturesque scenery.
